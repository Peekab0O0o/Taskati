Privacy Policy for Taskati

Last updated: 20/12/2024

At Taskati, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Taskati mobile application.

By using the App, you agree to the terms outlined below.

1. Information We Collect

Taskati is an offline-first application and does not automatically collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data. However, we may collect limited data if you voluntarily contact us:

  • Email Address: Collected when you send us an email through the app’s support feature.
  • Basic Device Information: Automatically included in emails to help us troubleshoot issues (e.g., device model, operating system version).

We do not access or collect any other personal or sensitive user data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the limited information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiries.
  • Resolve technical issues based on the information you include in your emails.

We do not use your information for advertising, marketing, or other purposes beyond supporting the app’s functionality.

3. Data Sharing

Your privacy is important to us. We do not share your information with any third parties.

4. Data Retention

  • Local Data: All app data is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to external servers.

If you delete tasks within the app, the data is removed from your device.

5. Security

Local Data: Since Taskati operates offline, the security of your data depends on your device’s built-in security measures (e.g., passcodes, biometrics).

We recommend enabling strong security settings on your device to safeguard your information.

6. Notifications

The app uses local notifications to remind you of task deadlines. These notifications are generated and processed entirely on your device, and we do not collect or transmit any notification-related data.

7. Your Rights

As the app operates offline, all data remains under your control. You have the following rights:

  • Access and Manage Data: You can view, edit, or delete tasks directly within the app.
  • Data Deletion: Deleting tasks will remove them from your device.
